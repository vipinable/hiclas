Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum personal data needed to operate the platform:

DataWhen collectedPurpose
Email addressWhen you sign inAuthentication and relay communications
Listing content (title, description, price, location, images)When you post a listingPublishing your ad to the marketplace
Relay messagesWhen you use Express Interest or reply via emailRouting private communications between users
Session tokenAfter sign-inKeeping you authenticated during a session
IP address & basic request metadataOn each page visitSecurity, fraud prevention, and abuse detection

We do not collect your name, phone number, postal address, payment information, device fingerprint, or browsing history.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the HighlyClassifieds service:

  • Authentication — your email address is used to send one-time login codes and to identify your account;
  • Listing publication — listing content you submit is displayed to other users on the platform;
  • Email relay — your email address is used as the destination for relay-forwarded messages from buyers or sellers you have interacted with;
  • Security — IP address and request data help us detect abuse, enforce rate limits, and investigate reports;
  • Service improvement — aggregated, anonymised usage patterns (not personal data) inform platform improvements.

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure:

  • Listing content and images are stored in Amazon S3 with server-side encryption and no public access;
  • User account data is managed by Amazon Cognito with industry-standard token security;
  • Listing metadata is stored in Amazon DynamoDB, encrypted at rest;
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.2 or higher.

Access to personal data is restricted to the systems and functions that need it to operate. No human operator has routine access to user data or message content.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes. Data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • AWS infrastructure — data is processed and stored on Amazon Web Services as our cloud provider;
  • Legal obligations — we may disclose data if required to do so by law, regulation, or valid legal process (e.g. a court order);
  • Safety — we may share information with law enforcement if we have a good-faith belief that disclosure is necessary to prevent imminent harm.

Email Relay & Privacy

The HighlyClassifieds email relay is a core privacy feature. When a buyer expresses interest in your listing, we notify you using a masked address for the buyer. Replies route through our system without either party’s real email address being revealed.

Relay messages are temporarily stored on our infrastructure for delivery. Once successfully delivered, messages are not retained beyond what is needed for abuse investigation. We may review relay content in response to a valid trust and safety report or legal requirement, but do not monitor relay traffic routinely.

Cookies & Tracking

HighlyClassifieds uses minimal client-side storage:

  • localStorage — your session token and email address are stored in your browser’s localStorage after you sign in. This data never leaves your device except as an authentication header when making API requests;
  • We do not use advertising cookies, third-party analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting technologies.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data (email address, Cognito identity) — retained until you request account deletion;
  • Listing content — retained until you remove the listing or request deletion;
  • Relay messages — retained for a maximum of 90 days after delivery, then automatically purged;
  • Access logs — retained for up to 30 days for security and abuse investigation purposes.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data;
  • Erasure — request deletion of your account and associated data;
  • Restriction — ask us to stop processing your data in certain circumstances;
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format;
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@highlyclassifieds.com from the address linked to your account. We will respond within 30 days.

Children’s Privacy

HighlyClassifieds is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact privacy@highlyclassifieds.com and we will delete the information promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the “Last updated” date at the top of this page will be revised. For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify registered users by email.

Continued use of the platform after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please contact our privacy team:

  • Email: privacy@highlyclassifieds.com
  • Response time: within 30 days for formal requests; typically within 2 business days for general enquiries.
