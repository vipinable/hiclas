About HighlyClassifieds

A privacy-first local marketplace connecting buyers and sellers in your community.

Our Mission

HighlyClassifieds was built on a simple belief: buying and selling locally should be easy, safe, and private. We connect people in their communities without ever exposing personal contact details — your email address stays yours.

Whether you're clearing out a garage, searching for a secondhand bargain, or growing a small business, HighlyClassifieds gives you a straightforward marketplace with no middleman fees and no data harvesting.

What We Offer

Free Listings

Post anything — vehicles, electronics, furniture, clothing and more — at no cost, with up to 10 photos per listing.

Private Messaging

Communicate with buyers and sellers through our masked email relay — neither party ever sees the other's real address.

Privacy First

Sign in with just your email and a one-time code — no passwords, no social logins, no tracking.

Always On

Built on AWS serverless infrastructure — fast, reliable, and available around the clock with no downtime windows.

How It Works

1

Sign in with your email

Enter your email address and we'll send a one-time 6-digit code. No password to remember, no account to create — just click "Login" and you're in.

2

Browse or post listings

Search thousands of local listings by keyword, category or location. Ready to sell? Post your item in under a minute with photos, a description and your asking price.

3

Connect privately

Spotted something you like? Hit "Express Interest" and we'll notify the seller on your behalf. They can reply directly to your masked address — and you to theirs — without either of you ever seeing a real email address.

4

Close the deal

Once you've agreed on price and pickup details over the relay, meet locally, inspect the item, and complete the transaction. Simple, safe, and community-driven.

Privacy & the Masked Email Relay

When you express interest in a listing, HighlyClassifieds sends a notification to the seller on your behalf. Any reply from the seller is routed through our relay back to your inbox — and vice versa. At no point does either party receive the other's real email address. Your identity stays private until you choose to share it.

Built on Solid Foundations

HighlyClassifieds runs entirely on AWS serverless services: S3 for storage, CloudFront for global delivery, DynamoDB for the listings database, Cognito for identity, Lambda for the API, and SES for email. There are no servers to patch, no databases to babysit, and the platform scales automatically with demand.
