Getting Started

What is HighlyClassifieds?

HighlyClassifieds is a free online marketplace where individuals can post and browse local classified ads. You can buy and sell almost anything — vehicles, electronics, furniture, clothing, real estate and more — all without paying listing fees or commissions.

A key feature is our privacy-first design: buyers and sellers communicate through a masked email relay so neither party ever sees the other's real email address.

Do I need to create an account?

You can browse all listings without an account. To post a listing or express interest in someone else's listing, you'll need to sign in.

Signing in is simple — just enter your email address and we'll send you a one-time 6-digit code. No password needed. Your account is created automatically the first time you sign in.

How does the one-time code login work?

Click "Login" in the top-right corner and enter your email address. We'll send a 6-digit code to that address within a few seconds. Enter the code in the app and you're signed in.

The code expires after 15 minutes. If it expires or you don't receive it, click "Resend code" to get a fresh one.

Is HighlyClassifieds available on mobile?

Yes — the site is fully responsive and works on any modern smartphone or tablet browser. There is no separate app to install; just open your browser and go.

Posting Listings

How do I post a listing?

Sign in, then click the blue "Post a Listing" button in the top-right corner. Fill in the title, price, category, location, and description. You can attach up to 10 photos by clicking the upload area or dragging files into it. When you're happy, click "Post listing" and your ad goes live immediately.

What categories are available?

Current categories include:

  • Vehicles
  • Real Estate
  • Electronics
  • Clothing & Fashion
  • Games & Hobbies
  • Books & Media
  • General

More categories are added regularly based on community demand.

How many images can I upload per listing?

You can upload up to 10 images per listing. Supported formats are JPEG, PNG, WebP, and GIF. Each image must be no larger than 10 MB. We recommend landscape-orientation photos for the best presentation in the gallery.

What items are prohibited?

The following are not permitted on HighlyClassifieds:

  • Illegal goods or services of any kind
  • Weapons, firearms, or ammunition (where restricted by law)
  • Counterfeit or stolen goods
  • Hazardous materials
  • Live animals (unless licensed breeders complying with local law)
  • Adult or explicit content
  • Personal data or financial instruments

Listings that violate these rules will be removed and the account may be suspended.

How long does a listing stay live?

Listings remain live indefinitely until you choose to remove them. We recommend closing or updating a listing once the item has been sold to keep the marketplace fresh for buyers.

Buying & Connecting

How do I contact a seller?

Open a listing and click the green "Express Interest" button. HighlyClassifieds will send an email notification to the seller on your behalf. The seller can then reply directly to your masked address, and the conversation continues through our relay without either of you seeing the other's real email.

You must be signed in to use this feature.

What exactly is the "Express Interest" button?

When you click "Express Interest", our system sends an email to the listing owner telling them someone is interested in their item. The email includes listing details and a masked reply-to address that routes to your inbox.

When the seller clicks Reply in their email client, their reply is delivered to your real inbox — but they never see your actual email address. The same applies in reverse when you reply back. The entire back-and-forth stays private.

Will the seller see my email address?

No. All communication goes through HighlyClassifieds' masked relay. The seller sees an @highlyclassifieds.com relay address, not your personal email. You only share your real contact details if you choose to include them in a message yourself.

Can I reply to a message from my regular email client?

Yes — that's the point. When you receive a forwarded message from HighlyClassifieds, just hit Reply in Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, or any other email app. Your reply will be routed through the relay automatically. No need to log back into the website to continue a conversation.

Does HighlyClassifieds handle payments or escrow?

No. HighlyClassifieds is a listing and communication platform only. Payments are arranged directly between buyers and sellers. We recommend meeting in a safe public location and inspecting items before paying. Prefer cash or trusted payment methods for local transactions.

Privacy & Security

What personal data does HighlyClassifieds collect?

We collect only the data needed to operate the platform:

  • Your email address (used for login and masked relay)
  • Listing content you publish (title, description, images, price, location)
  • Email messages exchanged through the relay (stored for delivery)

We do not collect your name, phone number, postal address, payment information, or browsing history.

How does the masked email relay protect my privacy?

Each user is assigned a unique relay address tied to their account identifier (not their real email). When you express interest in a listing, the seller receives a notification with your relay address as the reply-to. Replies route through our system to your real inbox, and vice versa, with real email addresses replaced throughout.

This means you can have a full conversation with a buyer or seller without either of you ever knowing the other's actual contact details.

How is my data stored and secured?

All data is stored on Amazon Web Services (AWS) in the us-east-1 region. S3 buckets are private, encrypted at rest, and accessible only through CloudFront or verified Lambda functions. Cognito manages authentication with industry-standard token security. Data in transit is always encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes. To request deletion of your account and associated data, email privacy@highlyclassifieds.com from the address linked to your account. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Account & Billing

Is HighlyClassifieds free?

Yes — entirely free for buyers and sellers. There are no listing fees, no success commissions, and no premium tiers. HighlyClassifieds is a community-focused platform that believes local commerce should be accessible to everyone.

How do I sign out?

Click the "Logout" button next to your email address in the top-right corner of any page. Your session token is removed from this browser immediately.

I didn't receive my login code. What do I do?

Check your spam or junk folder first — the code email comes from noreply@highlyclassifieds.com. If it's not there, click "Resend code" on the login screen to request a new one. Codes expire after 15 minutes so a fresh one may be needed.

If the problem persists, contact us at support@highlyclassifieds.com.

I found a listing that seems suspicious. How do I report it?

Email trust@highlyclassifieds.com with the listing URL and a brief description of your concern. Our team reviews all reports and acts within 24 hours. Please do not share personal financial details with anyone you haven't met in person.
